Please be aware Syphon is in Open Alpha.

Syphon is a privacy centric matrix chat client built on the foundations of privacy, branding, and user experience 
in an effort to pull others away from proprietary chat clients to the matrix protocol. 

Syphon is a not for profit effort, and will always be a community driven application with a mission to help others communicate freely.

Email us with any questions, concerns, or to get involved for hello@syphon.org.
